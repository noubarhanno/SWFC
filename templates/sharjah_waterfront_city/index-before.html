Azizi Riviera

Azizi Riviera is easily accessible via Business Bay, Sheikh Zayed Road, Al Khail Road and Meydan Road. It is also well-connected with a proposed metro line, malls, cinemas, retail, schools, hospitals and other modern necessities. The Meydan Hotel and Racecourse are a few minutes away, and so is the proposed One Mall and marina.

The first phase of Azizi Riviera is set within a multiphase development comprising of 18 mid-rise residential buildings comprising 2,273 units , a mega integrated retail district, waterfront views and lush greenery.
